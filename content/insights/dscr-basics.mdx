**DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) = NOI / Debt Service.**

- Aim for >1.25 in base case; stress to ~1.1 with lower occupancy and higher expenses.
- Sensitivity table: occupancy × ADR × expenses.
- Watch local taxes/HOA and insurance shocks specifically.

_AI helps generate the sensitivity grid fast; humans decide on thresholds._
